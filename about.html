Get to Know Me

A Journey Shaped by Strength

My journey into Machine Learning and Data Science began with curiosity — a desire to understand how data can be transformed into meaningful insights and intelligent solutions. With a strong academic foundation in Computer Science, what started as learning programming fundamentals gradually evolved into exploring advanced machine learning algorithms and predictive modeling.

Strength & Fortitude

I was raised in India as a single child. In 2014, I lost my father at the age of twelve, a moment that profoundly shaped my perspective on life. My mother carried every responsibility with remarkable determination, teaching me resilience, discipline, and the value of hard work.

These early experiences cultivated a mindset of problem-solving and persistence — qualities that naturally guided me toward Machine Learning. I was drawn to the field because it combines analytical reasoning and pattern recognition — skills I had been practicing in life long before I wrote my first line of code.

"I believe challenges are like data points: they teach, refine, and strengthen you."

Moving to the United States in 2024 to pursue my Master’s degree further strengthened my independence and adaptability — traits I now bring to every project, every dataset, and every model I build. Today, I combine technical expertise with resilience, striving to build intelligent solutions that create meaningful impact.
